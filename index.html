* Porsche is a prestigious German automobile manufacturer specializing in high-performance sports cars, SUVs, and sedans. Founded in 1931 by Ferdinand Porsche, the brand is renowned for its iconic 911 model, along with other popular models like the 718, Panamera, Macan, Cayenne, and Taycan. With strong ideals and a rich history, Porsche stands as a symbol of luxury and performance in the automotive world.

* BMW, or Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, is a German multinational corporation that produces luxury vehicles and motorcycles. Founded in 1916, BMW is known for its performance and quality, operating over 30 production sites worldwide. It’s a leader in premium car and motorcycle manufacturing, committed to innovation and sustainability.

* Audi, a subsidiary of Volkswagen AG, is renowned for its premium cars and motorcycles. With a legacy dating back to the 19th century, Audi embodies “Vorsprung durch Technik,” signifying a commitment to high quality and progressive design. As a global leader in the premium and supercar segment, Audi continues to shape the future of mobility.

* Dodge, an iconic American automobile brand, is renowned for its performance cars. Established by the Dodge brothers in the early 1900s, it became part of Chrysler and now operates under Stellantis North America. Dodge’s legacy includes classic muscle cars like the Charger and Challenger.
